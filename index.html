The Changing Court

Over the years, the way in which ‘Religious Freedom’ has been interpreted by the Courts has evolved and shifted, moving through distinct ‘Constituitional Landscapes’, as discussed in class. Through this site, I’ve examined two periods in particular – the Second Landscape from 1840 to 1940, and the Third Landscape, from 1940 until the present day. Through this site, a timeline can be constructed, creating a visual element through which the differing views of religion can be seen.

Why this project?

To showcase the changing nature of the Court in an easy to understand, visualized nature using technology as a tool.
